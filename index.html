About Me

I'm a 3rd Year student in ATU college Galway. I'm currently studying Computing and Digital media.

I've always had an interest in creative mediums. From a young age, art, music and movies have always been a passion of mine. As a child I took an interest in photographing people and things. Usually at family events I would take the camera from my Dad and start taking pictures, this was my first exposure to photography.


It wasn't until I hit my teens that my love for photography was fully realised. I discovered photographers on instagram and saw how impressive their photos were compared to the ones I took. I downloaded Adobe Lightroom and I spent some time learning how to take photos and how to edit them.


After some time of practicing and posting to my Instagram, I created my Facebook page and started taking photos and comissions. It's been some time since I've had my Facebook page active but I'm now open for hire and prints again.
